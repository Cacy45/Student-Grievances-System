Welcome to DUT Finance Grievance System

At DUT, we understand that financial challenges can affect your academic journey. This platform is designed to help students address any grievances related to finances in a simple and effective way.

Whether it's an issue with your bursary, tuition payments, or financial aid, our grievance system allows you to report, track, and resolve these concerns swiftly. Your satisfaction and well-being are our top priority!

About Us

The DUT Finance Grievance System was established to provide a transparent and efficient way for students to address financial concerns. Our aim is to ensure that every student receives the support they need to manage their academic finances effectively.

We are committed to resolving issues regarding tuition fees, financial aid, bursaries, and other financial matters that may arise during your academic career. Our platform offers a simple interface for students to file grievances, track their progress, and communicate with the relevant department for a quick resolution.

The system ensures that all grievances are handled professionally, ensuring that every student's voice is heard and acted upon.
