Welcome to the DUT Student Grievance System

About Us

The DUT Student Grievance System ensures students can report issues related to academics, administration, campus facilities, and more. We aim to provide a structured and fair resolution process for all student concerns.

How It Works

Follow these steps to resolve your concerns efficiently:

  1. Submit a grievance: Provide detailed information and any supporting documents.
  2. Track progress: Monitor the status of your grievance through the dashboard.
  3. Receive resolution: Get notified when a decision is made and take further action if necessary.
